Instructions to play Handcricket

  • The game is played between two players, a batsman and a bowler.
  • The batsman and bowler simultaneously choose a number between 1 and 6.
  • If the numbers match, the batsman is out.
  • If the numbers do not match, then the batsman scores the number they selected.
  • This process continues until the batsman gets out.
  • The player with more runs will win the game.
